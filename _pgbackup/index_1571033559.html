Doral Academy Zeta Omicron

Doral Academy's Computer Science club was founded in 2015 under our club sponsor Prof. Crudele who has nurtured the club members to become state champions before the end of the decade. Our beloved Prof supported the club members and, in turn, some members like our former president Pedro Contipelli helped take the club to the next level. This website is in dedication to the wonderful leaders who passed through this club and gave back to it.
