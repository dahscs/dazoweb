Doral Academy Zeta Omicron Society

Doral Academy's Computer Science club was founded in 2015 by our club sponsor Prof. Crudele, who nurtured the club and made it's members state champions before the end of the decade. Our beloved Prof heavily supported his kids and, in turn, the club members worked to make this club an amazing place for everyone. This website is in dedication to the wonderful leaders who passed through this club and gave back to it, changing us for the better.
