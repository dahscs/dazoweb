Doral Academy Zeta Omicron

Doral Academy's Computer Science club was founded in 2015 by it's first President Vicente and our club sponsor Prof. Crudele, who nurtured the club and made it's members state champions before the end of the decade. Our beloved Prof heavily supported his kids and, in turn, club members like our former president Pedro Contipelli helped take the club to the next level. This website is in dedication to the wonderful leaders who passed through this club and gave back to it, changing us for the better.
